The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Your Name

This is one my favourite anime movies, the art style and everything about the movie is just really good.

Bullet Train

I enjoyed this movie a lot, there were so many small details that were not overlooked e.g the water bottle :), the japanese version of staying alive was fun to listen to.

The Kingsmen (all of the movies)

Honestly I could watch any of the movies directed by Matthew Vaughn just because all of his movies (that I have seen so far) have been really good, the action, the story and the playful acts that sometimes happen in his movie style is really the best.
